# World Code Addon Skills Roadmap

Every skill reads from the user's `world-code/` files (climax, method, creation, conversation, crossing) so output is never generic — it's tailored to their world.

## Business Model

- **World Code Pack** = free/cheap foundation that creates the `world-code/` files
- **Addon Skill Packs** = paid products, each a standalone purchase

---

## Offer Lab Pack
- **Offer Architect** — Design a complete offer (promise, deliverables, pricing, bonuses) built around your climax and method
- **Offer Stack Builder** — Create tiered offer structures (free → low → mid → high ticket) that ladder people through your world
- **Pricing Strategy** — Determine pricing based on your transformation promise and market positioning
- **Bonus Creator** — Generate strategic bonuses that reinforce your method and overcome objections
- **Offer Teardown** — Analyze an existing offer against your World Code and find the gaps
