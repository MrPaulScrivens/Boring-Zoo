## Hook Formulas — World Code Style

The first line determines whether anyone reads the rest. Pull hooks from your World Code tension.

### Climax-Powered Hooks
- "I was wrong about [Wrong Belief from conversation.md]."
- "[Before State pain] isn't the real problem."
- "The real reason [Before State frustration] happens isn't what you think."
- "From [Before State] to [After State] — and it started with [Method principle]."

### Method-Powered Hooks
- "Most people [common approach]. Here's why [Method Name] works differently:"
- "Stop [Before State behavior]. Do this instead:"
- "The [Method Name] approach to [Conversation theme]:"
- "[Number] [Method principles] that [Climax promise]:"

### Voice-Powered Hooks
- Use the Authenticity Markers from `voice.md` to create hooks that sound unmistakably like you
- Lead with your natural Tone & Character
- Break conventional patterns using your Hard Rules

### Story Hooks
- "Last week, [experience related to your World]."
- "3 years ago, I was [Before State]. Today, [After State]."
- "I almost [big mistake related to Wrong Belief]."

## Content Repurposing System

Turn one piece of content into many:

### Blog Post → Social Content

| Platform | Format |
|----------|--------|
| LinkedIn | Key insight from Conversation theme + Method takeaway |
| Twitter/X | Thread breaking down Method phases |
| Instagram | Carousel of transformation steps |

### Repurposing Workflow

1. **Create pillar content** around Conversation themes
2. **Extract key insights** (3-5 per piece)
3. **Adapt to each platform** (format and tone, always in Voice)
4. **Schedule across the week** per Platform/Rhythm
5. **Update and reshare** evergreen World Code content

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Engagement Routine (30 min)

1. Respond to all comments on your posts (5 min)
2. Comment on 5-10 posts from target accounts (15 min)
3. Share/repost with added insight using your Voice (5 min)
4. Send 2-3 DMs to new connections (5 min)

### Quality Comments (in your Voice)

- Add new insight from your Method perspective
- Share a related experience from your World
- Ask a thoughtful question from Conversation themes
- Respectfully disagree using your worldview

## Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics That Matter

**Awareness:** Impressions, Reach, Follower growth rate
**Engagement:** Engagement rate, Comments, Shares/reposts, Saves
**Conversion:** Link clicks, Profile visits, DMs received, Leads to Crossing Entry Point

### Weekly Review

- Top 3 performing posts — which World Code element resonated?
- Bottom 3 posts — was it off-Voice? Off-theme?
- Which Conversation theme gets most engagement?
- Which hook style works best?
